About Stitched by Gigi

Hi and welcome to my shop! Sewing my art aprons started as gifts for relatives and friends and with their encouragement, I decided to sell them, and this little shop was born. Since then my shop items have grown and I continue to add more items.

My appliqued pieces are all hand drawn and hand cut and machine appliqued on the aprons, bibs, onesies etc.

I love finding interesting appealing fabrics that I hope my customers enjoy. Creating my items brings me a lot of personal satisfaction and watching my business grow is one of my favorite parts of sewing.

I have so many goals for myself and I am so grateful that I have this outlet and YOU to allow me to create!

Don't hesitate to send me a note if you would like a custom fabric or theme you would like me to use, or if you have an all-new idea.
